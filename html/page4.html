CO2 Emission

As we all know, CO2, as one of the most important anthropogenic Greenhouse Gases, can be produced by the burning of fossil fuels. With the increase of CO2 in atmosphere, the positive Greenhouse effect will heat the troposphere air temperature, which will cause more extremely heat wave and global warming trend. Iceberg in high latitude will melt under global warming, which favors sea-surface-level rising. Low altitude islands may suffer unbearable disaster at that moment.
