Introduction

D3 Force

Energy is critical to economic development. It is the locomotive that drives the process of industrialization. Human beings have used various types of energy in the past 400 years, which has revolutionized their society in many ways. With the continuous development of the economy, the prosperity and well-being of human beings have also been greatly improved. This, in turn, has stimulated further demand for energy services in areas such as lighting, heating and cooking, communications and transportation, and the demand for energy itself. For many developing countries, this process is in the ascendant; for others, it is still just a luxury.
